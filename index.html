◈ WHAT IS FFPL

A league built for serious architects

FFPL is the governing body for competitive Formula Front: Extreme Battle on PSP. This site is League HQ — the official record of standings, sanctioned events, and commissioner decisions.

Players participate through Architect Nexus — the dedicated client that reads your save file, verifies your AC loadout, and connects you to the league server. Matches run through PPSSPP with full replay capture. Standings use Glicko-2, updated after every sanctioned result.

◈ FEATURES

Everything the circuit needs

🔒

VERIFIED LOADOUTS

Architect Nexus reads your PPSSPP save file directly. Part hashes are extracted, tamper-checked, and bound to your architect account — no manual entry.

📈

GLICKO-2 RANKED LADDER

Rating, deviation, and volatility updated after every sanctioned match. The ladder reflects true competitive form, not just win count.

CHALLENGE SYSTEM

Issue ranked challenges to any architect within your standing range. Anti-padding rules prevent griefing. Vacation mode protects inactive architects.

◈

COMMISSIONER EVENTS

Licensed commissioners run sanctioned tournaments and issue league announcements. Dispute resolution is handled through the HQ tools with full match record access.

🎥

REPLAY ARCHIVE

Every match replay is captured and optionally published. Architects control their own replay visibility from their profile page.

ARCHITECT NEXUS CLIENT

The Architect Nexus desktop app orchestrates PPSSPP directly — launching instances, tiling spectator windows, and reporting match results to the FFPL server automatically. A web client is also available.

◈ HOW IT WORKS

From save file to standings in four steps

01

REGISTER YOUR ARCHITECT PROFILE

Create an account in the HQ portal. Set your handle, region, and challenge mode.

02

SYNC YOUR ARMORED CORE

Open Architect Nexus (desktop or web), drop in your PPSSPP save file, and your AC loadout is extracted, hashed, and bound to your architect account automatically.

03

ISSUE OR ACCEPT CHALLENGES

Find an opponent on the leaderboard and issue a ranked challenge. Challenges are time-limited and expire if not answered. Anti-padding rules keep it fair.

04

RUN THE MATCH

Architect Nexus launches both AC instances in PPSSPP, captures the result, and posts it to the FFPL server. Ratings update immediately on the league leaderboard.
