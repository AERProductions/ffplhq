◈ WHAT IS FFPL

Build. Tune. Compete.

Formula Front Pro League is the official ranked circuit for Armored Core: Formula Front — Extreme Battle on PSP — the only AC game where you program your AC's AI and send it into battle without touching a controller. You design the machine, train the AI, and watch it win or lose on its own merits.

Upload your save, register your team through Architect Nexus, and issue ranked challenges. When a challenge is accepted, the match runs automatically on the league server — no scheduling required, no live session. Standings use Glicko-2, updated after every sanctioned result.

◈ GET SET UP

Getting started with FFPL

All you need is your PSP or PPSSPP save file and a league account. Upload your save, register your loadout, and you're on the ladder.

01
Create your HQ account
Register at ffplhq.com/hq/ with an email and password. This is your architect profile — it holds your challenge mode, Glicko-2 rating, and full match history.
Free Account
02
Upload your save file
In the Hangar tab, upload your PSP or PPSSPP save file. Your AC loadout is read from the save, verified, and registered to your architect profile automatically. US, JP, and EU saves are all supported.
Hangar Tab
03
Set your challenge mode
In the Profile tab, set your challenge mode to Open to appear on the leaderboard and accept incoming challenges. Use Vacation mode to pause without affecting your rating deviation.
Profile Tab
04

Find an architect on the Leaderboard within your standing range and send a ranked challenge. Once accepted, the match runs automatically on the league server — no live session needed. Ratings update immediately when results are posted.

◈ HOW IT WORKS

From save file to standings in four steps

01

REGISTER YOUR ARCHITECT PROFILE

Create an account in the HQ portal. Set your handle, region, and challenge mode.

02

UPLOAD YOUR SAVE FILE

In the Hangar tab, upload your PSP or PPSSPP save file. Your AC loadout is read, verified, and registered to your profile automatically. US, JP, and EU saves are all supported.

03

ISSUE OR ACCEPT CHALLENGES

Find an opponent on the leaderboard and issue a ranked challenge. Challenges are time-limited and expire if not answered. Anti-padding rules keep it fair.

04

MATCH RUNS — RATINGS UPDATE

Once a challenge is accepted, both AC loadouts are queued on the league server. The match runs automatically — no live session, no scheduling. Results post and Glicko-2 ratings update immediately.
